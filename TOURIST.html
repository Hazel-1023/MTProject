ENGKANTADORA

An enchanting waterfall located in the mountainous village of Dipilat in Vintar, Ilocos Norte. The Engkantadora waterfall named after its enchanting natural ambience, is approximately 18 kilometers from this city or just a 30-minute drive from the Vintar municipal hall.

SOLCOK

Marvel at its pristine waters cascading gracefully over rugged cliffs, forming a serene natural basin perfect for cooling off beneath the tropical canopy. This magnificent waterfall offers both a rejuvenating getaway for thrill-seekers and a sanctuary that nurtures the area's vibrant wildlife and natural heritage.

UBBOG NI ALSEM

Ubbog ti Alsem is a river paradise and popular tourist spot in the municipality of Vintar, Ilocos Norte, known for its crystal-clear waters and natural beauty. Visitors go there to swim, relax, and enjoy the serene and picturesque surroundings.

VINTAR DAM

Vintar Dam is a recreational and irrigation site in Vintar, Ilocos Norte, famous for its crystal-clear waters and activities like swimming, picnicking, and adventurous sports like ziplining, zip biking, and kayaking.

UPPER DAM

Upper Dam Vintar is a popular tourist destination in Vintar, Ilocos Norte, known for its river, recreational activities, and scenic views. The area is generally free to enter, with fees for activities or cottage rentals.

PALLAS VIEWDECK

Pallas Viewdeck in Vintar, Ilocos Norte offers a tranquil and awe-inspiring "sea of clouds" experience, especially during sunrise. Located in the Pallas Valley, it's known for its breathtaking panoramic views, particularly from the vantage point in Brgy. 14 Ester.
